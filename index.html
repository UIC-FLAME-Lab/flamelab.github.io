Welcome To The Flame Lab

Memories not only record our past experiences, shaping our identity, but also serve as "building blocks" for imagination, reasoning, and creativity. Using Behavioral and neuroimaging methods, we study how memory improvements support these other abilities. Our goal is to understand their developement, function, and intersection, and to leverage this knowledge to promote positive outcomes for children from diverse backgrounds.
