POTENTIAL UNDERGRADUATE RESEARCH ASSISTANTS

We invite UIC undergraduate students to apply to join the lab as research assistants. Some of the benefits of working in the lab are receiving hands-on research experience related to the neurocognitive development of episodic memory and future imagination, as well as obtaining guidance on how to navigate the graduate school application process. As a research assistant, you can expect to:

  • Maintain regular weekly hours in the lab
  • Attend and participate in weekly lab meetings
  • Attend and participate in journal club meetings

If you would like to be considered for an RA position in our lab, please fill out our application and email it to our Lab Manager, Sinem Camlica at scamlica@uic.edu.
